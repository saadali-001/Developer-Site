Who We Are : What is Our Moto

ABC is a Registered Educational and Community Welfare Organization established in the year 2019, dedicated towards serving the underprivileged amongst the Indian community primarily focused on candidates in India. For the last 3 years ABC has contributed significantly towards the overall educational, socio-economic development of backward communities in India.


Since its inception Trust has been at the forefront to address the socio-economic issues and educational backwardness of the Minority community from the grass rot levels. In fact, ABC seldom refers to itself as a charitable organization; rather it prefers to be acknowledged as a community service organization.
