Our Programs

I want to share my programs with everyone


Since its inception Trust has been at the forefront to address the socio-economic issues and educational backwardness of the Minority community from the grass rot levels. In fact, ABC seldom refers to itself as a charitable organization; rather it prefers to be acknowledged as a community service organization.


Our agenda has been very clear all these years – empower youngsters, widows, orphans and slum dwellers with self-employment & job skills, quality education for children and free health treatment for the poor. Over the last two and a half decades ABC has been successful in establishing 5 well known Trainers & many more vocational trainers at A Best classes for every child to do after school programs.


Academic Enrichment

Mission

To eradicate ignorance, illiteracy & poverty from amongst the underprivileged Indian Community through education and vocational training, Self-employment Programs, Micro finance interest free loans and Rozgar centers.


Classroom Activities

Children learn how to independently manage their time for both short-term and long-term assignments as they develop study skills and organizational skills. Achievement and effort grades are given in the four core subjects of Language Arts, Math, Science, and Social Studies. Fifth and sixth grade students are part of student-led parent conferences each year so they can join in on the discussion to support their own learning.

Elementary Programs

Specialist classes enrich and extend our students’ daily experience, incuding regular visits to the library, Art and Music, and Physical Education. At the conclusion of sixth grade, CEE students are well-prepared and ready to continue their self-exploration as learners, thriving in their new secondary school environments.

Value Added Programs

The Value Added programs at ABC are an integral part of the curriculum and gives students strong acceleration with regards to their employability. The selected certifications are considered after rigorous discussions with industry personnel. These Certificate programs are required for the students to function in the industry as a part of Regulators directive.
